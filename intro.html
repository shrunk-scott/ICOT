The International Conference of Thinking (ICOT) is a global forum dedicated to the exploration, development and advancement of human thinking.

ICOT brings together educators, researchers, innovators and thought leaders from around the world to examine how thinking shapes learning, creativity, decision-making and problem-solving across disciplines.

Through dialogue, research and shared practice, ICOT aims to foster deeper, more intentional thinking that empowers individuals and societies to navigate complexity, uncertainty and change.

Thinking is not just a skill — it is the foundation of progress.
